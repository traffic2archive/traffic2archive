How these exchange platforms work

  • Earn credits: You perform actions (follow, like, view) on other users' content to collect credits.
  • Redeem credits: Spend credits to have others follow/like/view your content.

Best practices & safety

  • Don't automate: use human-driven exchanges rather than bots to avoid platform penalties.

  • Target relevance: niche followers stick around longer than broad, untargeted followers.
