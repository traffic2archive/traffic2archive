Quick Action Plan (3 steps)

  1. Pick 1–2 tools: Use them for a 7–10 day push to measure lift.
  2. Target content: Promote 1 video/post per campaign — keep creative consistent.
  3. Follow up organically: Reply to new followers, run a poll, or pin a welcomed comment to keep retention high.

Safety checklist

  • Always avoid automation (bots) — use human-exchange systems.
  • Don’t suddenly spike volumes at unnatural rates — spread activity across days.
  • Respect platform rules for follow/unfollow limits and engagement actions.
